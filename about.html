Who we are

Building something worth believing in

We started with a simple idea and grew it into something bigger. Here's the story of how we got here — and where we're headed.

01 — Our story

Where it all began

It started in a small room with two people, a whiteboard, and an audacious belief that things could be done differently. Founded in 1985, we took our first steps without a roadmap — only a conviction that the problem was worth solving. That spark became our foundation.

02 — Our growth

Years of steady progress

Growth rarely arrives in a straight line. We scaled through challenges, pivoted when the market demanded it, and doubled down when the signals were right. Each year brought new lessons and new milestones that pushed our ambitions higher.
